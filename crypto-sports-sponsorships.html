Fan Token Penetration — Socios / Chiliz (Big 5 Leagues)

Chiliz (CHZ) fan tokens via Socios.com represent the most widespread crypto-to-sports integration by club count. Penetration varies sharply by league: Serie A adopted most aggressively via a league-level deal in 2021; the Bundesliga is a near-total outlier driven by fan resistance. Tokens that launched but have lost 90%+ of value are technically live but commercially dormant. Data as of April 2026.

League Clubs Penetration Active Tokens Notes
Premier League 20 ~35% 7 / 20 Man City, Arsenal, Tottenham, Everton, Aston Villa, Crystal Palace, Leeds (relegated). Strong uptake across large and mid-tier clubs.
Serie A 20 ~30-35% 6-7 / 20 Juventus, AC Milan, Inter, Roma, Napoli, Lazio, Bologna. Strongest Big-5 uptake; driven by a league-level Socios deal signed in 2021.
La Liga 20 ~20% 4 / 20 Barcelona, Atletico Madrid, Valencia, Levante (relegated). LaLiga also has a league-level Socios partnership but penetration is lower than Serie A.
Ligue 1 18 ~6-11% 1-2 / 18 PSG confirmed; Lyon uncertain. Rest of the league absent. Ligue 1 clubs below PSG lack the international fanbase to generate meaningful token volume.
Bundesliga 18 ~0-5% 0-1 / 18 Neither Bayern nor BVB have official Socios tokens. BVB explicitly shelved its token after fan resistance. The Bundesliga's fan ownership culture (50+1 rule) and active supporter groups are structurally hostile to financialised fan products.

Source: Socios.com / Chiliz network data. Penetration calculated as clubs with active token launches as a share of total league clubs. Tokens with 90%+ value decline from peak classified as commercially dormant.
